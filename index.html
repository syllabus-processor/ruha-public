EMA

Electronic Mental Health Application

EMA is a clinician-centered software platform designed to reduce administrative burden in mental health practice.

Built for

  • Solo practitioners
  • Early-career therapists
  • Supervisors and training contexts

EMA prioritizes

  • Calm cognition
  • Ethical documentation
  • Clinician autonomy
  • Privacy by design

EMA does not

  • Market to patients
  • Sell or analyze clinical data
  • Use session content for AI training
  • Optimize for productivity metrics
This platform is currently in staged development and observation.
