SMS Notification Opt-In

EMA (Electronic Mental Health Application) uses SMS messaging only for account security and appointment-related notifications.

By providing your mobile number, you consent to receive SMS messages from EMA related to account security and appointment notifications. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

This page demonstrates the SMS opt-in workflow for carrier compliance review.
