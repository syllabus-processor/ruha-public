Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Ruha operates clinical software systems with a commitment to minimizing data collection, reducing exposure, and respecting the boundaries of mental health care.

We take privacy seriously and collect only what is necessary for the operation of the system.

What We Collect

We collect only the information required to operate our systems, such as:

  • Account information (email address, authentication data)
  • System configuration and scheduling metadata
  • Clinical documentation entered intentionally by a clinician
  • Audit logs required for security and compliance

Clinical data is handled only as necessary to provide functionality and is never used for advertising, analytics, or secondary purposes.

What We Do Not Do

We do not:

  • Sell or rent data
  • Use clinical data for advertising
  • Use session content to train AI models
  • Perform behavioral analytics on clinicians or clients
  • Record or transcribe telehealth sessions by default

Telehealth & Communications

Where communications features are present:

  • Sessions are ephemeral by default
  • No audio or video is recorded unless explicitly initiated
  • No background telemetry is collected

SMS Messaging

If you opt in to receive SMS messages:

  • Messages are strictly transactional (e.g., login codes, appointment notifications)
  • No clinical or PHI content is sent via SMS
  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP

Data Ownership & Access

  • Clinicians retain full ownership of their data
  • Data export is always available
  • Self-hosting options exist for qualified users

Security

We use encryption, access controls, and audit logging consistent with HIPAA-aligned practices. Access to data is restricted by role and logged for accountability.
